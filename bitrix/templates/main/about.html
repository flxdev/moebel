Stages of production

01
drawing

To get started on the surface of the material used all the parts are distributed, provided the design, then there is sawing the material on a special high-performance machine. Modern equipment provides for the scoring saw that provides cutting frequency and, as a conse-quence, the complete elimination of chipping and scuffing.

02
Level of production

From the details that have been pre-prepared, at this stage, the assembly of components. Then complete units will be assembled into a whole product. At this stage it is important to adherence of the whole sequence of operations performed. And only in this case, maintaining the strict furniture assembly technology, it will guaran-tee high quality of finished products.

03
Fittings

Our employees perform the packaging of furniture quickly and professionally. If you prefer to pack your own, we will deliver in advance everything you need (for a few hours or days before the arrival and early movers moving).
